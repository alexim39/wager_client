Coin-out Plan

A plan made for people who need short time investment scheme

You CashOut twice in 30 days

You get 2% of your investment each day except sundays

Your account gets credited at most 48 hours after your withdrawal request except on weekends

You Make CashOut investment twice (2 times) in a month

Invest a minimum of ₦5,000.00 and CashOut 2% of your invested amount daily for a week.

You get 2% return on your investment plus your initial investment after a week

 Coin-up Plan

A plan made for people who need long time investment scheme

You CashUp at the end of your chosen investment period

You get 1% of your investment each day except sundays

Your account gets credited at most 48 hours after your withdrawal request except on weekends

Invest a minimum of ₦3,000.00 and CashUp 1% every 24 hours.

You can request withdrawal at the expiration of investement period or re-invest from your balance

You get 1% return on your investment every 24 hours plus your initial investment
